Criteria of Miss Rwanda

Qualifying for Miss Rwanda

  1. Rwandan by nationality (ID Card or Passport)
  2. Aspirants should between 18 and 24 years of age
  3. Secondary school graduate
  4. Fluent in Kinyarwanda and any other language between French, English and Swahili
  5. Minimum height: 1,70 m
  6. Body Mass Index (BMI): 18.5 - 24.9
  7. Have no child by birth
  8. Ready to stay in Rwanda at least for one year if elected
  9. Must not get married during her reign
  10. Ready to represent Rwanda wherever and whenever needed
  11. Ready to follow all crowned Miss Rwanda rules and regulations
